Project Ideas

  • Word-by-Word Translation for less used languages: Google Translate is great, but only works with some of the world's languages. For most languages, you are forced to look up each translation manually. A tool that helps decyphering foreign text at least a bit might be helpful.
  • Sudoku-Solver: Rather silly idea, but a scope I could manage on the side. Maybe implement as a "Web Service"
  • Volume/Loudness measurement as Android App: Certainly already exists if at all possible.
  • Android App to show information based on GPS coordinates: Take data from Wikipedia or similar sources. Should work offline!
